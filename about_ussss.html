Introduction


Happy Coding Cafe was founded in 2022 by four co-founders; Lily Ching, Mars NG, Double, Alison Yan.

A good working environment and delicious food to enjoy what are uplifting. Programmers face complex coding on a daily basis, which was a necessity for the founders, who were also programmers. Therefore, we set up the Happy Coding Cafe to provide a place for people who need to face complicated work every day to feel happy. Happy programming cafe is a place where people can be happy whether you are at work or on a happy holiday.

Let's have fun!
