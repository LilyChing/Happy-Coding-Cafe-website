Introduction


Happy Coding Cafe was founded in 2022 by four co-founders; Lily Ching, Mars NG, Double, Alison Yan.

A good working environment and delicious food to enjoy what are uplifting. Programmers face complex coding on a daily basis, which was a necessity for the founders, who were also programmers. Therefore, we set up the Happy Coding Cafe to provide a place for people who need to face complicated work every day to feel happy. Happy programming cafe is a place where people can be happy whether you are at work or on a happy holiday.

Source of Food

Our food is carefully selected in order to give the best experience to those who enjoy it here. We use grass-fed cattle from Australia, the meat is lean and low in fat, the meat is firm and chewy.Salmon comes from the Norwegian Atlantic . It's clean and tasty. It's high in protein and omega 3, and it's one of the best ingredients in the world. In addition, we use dairy products and cilantro from Italy, such as creamy cheese and refreshing basil. We carefully select and strictly control the quality of food ingredients in order to bring you the best enjoyment.
